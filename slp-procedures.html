Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

Thrive Speech Therapy provides a PTO benefit to help all employees maintain a healthy work-life balance. This policy outlines how PTO is accrued, requested, and managed — giving you the flexibility to use your time for vacation, illness, appointments, and personal needs.

Key Highlights

  • ✓PTO accrues each pay period and can be used for vacation, illness, personal business, medical appointments, and emergencies.
  • ✓All PTO requests must be submitted through Gusto and approved before time off is taken.
  • ✓Carry-over is capped at 24 hours year-over-year.
  • ✓Unused PTO is not paid out upon resignation, termination, or end of employment.
  • ✓PTO requests should be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance for planned absences when possible.

Work from Home Policy

Thrive's Work from Home policy outlines the conditions under which remote work may be approved for eligible staff members. This policy is designed to maintain clinical quality while supporting flexibility for our team when operationally feasible.

Key Highlights

  • ✓Remote work arrangements must be pre-approved by clinic leadership and are not guaranteed for all positions or situations.
  • ✓Teletherapy sessions must comply with all HIPAA requirements including secure platforms and private environments.
  • ✓Clinicians working remotely must maintain the same professionalism, responsiveness, and clinical quality as in-clinic sessions.
  • ✓All documentation must be completed within the required timeframes regardless of work location.

Conflict Resolution Policy

At Thrive, we believe that a healthy, respectful workplace is the foundation of exceptional client care. Our Conflict Resolution Policy provides a clear, fair, and supportive process for addressing workplace disagreements in a way that honors everyone involved.

Our Approach

  • ✓All team members are encouraged to address conflicts directly and respectfully with the other party first, when safe to do so.
  • ✓If direct resolution is not possible, concerns should be escalated to your clinic supervisor or HR.
  • ✓All conflict resolution discussions are treated with confidentiality and handled with care and professionalism.
  • ✓Retaliation of any kind against someone who raises a concern in good faith is not tolerated at Thrive.
