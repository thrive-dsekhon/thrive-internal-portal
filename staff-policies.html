Got Questions?

Common Questions

Quick answers to the questions our team and families ask most often.

Thrive Speech Therapy requires a minimum of 2 hours notice before a scheduled session to cancel without penalty. Cancellations made with less than 2 hours notice or no-shows may be subject to a late cancellation fee. This policy exists to respect our clinicians' time and allow other families on our waitlist to access care. Please refer to the No Show / Late Cancel Policy document for complete details.

View Full Policy →

No-shows or cancellations with less than 2 hours notice are documented and may result in a late cancellation fee per our policy. Repeated no-shows may affect a client's scheduling priority. Our team will always reach out to support families in maintaining consistent attendance, as regular attendance is critical to achieving the best therapy outcomes for each child.

View Attendance Policy →

Thrive observes several major holidays throughout the year during which clinics are closed. Staff should refer to the Holiday Calendar & Policy document for the complete list of observed holidays, clinic closure dates, and guidelines for requesting time off. All time-off requests must be submitted and approved in advance through Gusto.

View Holiday Policy →

Any workplace injury should be reported immediately to your clinic supervisor and HR. Thrive's Workers' Compensation Policy outlines the complete reporting process, timeline requirements, and your rights as an employee. Please do not delay reporting — timely reporting ensures you receive the benefits and support you are entitled to. Review the Workers' Comp Guide for Thrivers for step-by-step instructions.

View Workers' Comp Documents →

During severe weather events — including hurricanes, flooding, or other Houston-area weather emergencies — Thrive will communicate clinic closure decisions through official company channels as early as possible. Families and staff should refer to the Severe Weather Cancellation Policy for complete procedures, including how sessions are rescheduled and how fees are handled during weather-related closures.

View Weather Policy →

For questions about any Thrive policy, please reach out to your clinic supervisor or contact our admin team directly. We are always here to help clarify and support you.
