For Multilingual Families

Bilingual Speech Development

Being bilingual or multilingual is a gift, not a barrier. Research clearly shows that learning two or more languages does not cause speech delays. At Thrive, we celebrate multilingual families and provide therapy that honors every language your child speaks.

The resources below are specifically for families raising bilingual or multilingual children — addressing common myths and providing practical guidance.

🌎 ASHA — Bilingualism & Children → 📖 Raising a Bilingual Child — Zero to Three →

💡 Common Myths About Bilingual Children

✗
Myth: Speaking two languages confuses children and causes delays.
✓
Fact: Bilingual children develop at the same rate as monolingual children when all languages are counted together.
✗
Myth: You should only speak one language at home if your child has a speech delay.
✓
Fact: Maintaining home languages is important for cultural identity and does not worsen speech delays.
